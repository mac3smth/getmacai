Mac's Process

The audit call.
Show up prepared. Let the demo do the selling.

Three phases. Total time: 20–30 minutes.

🔍
Phase 1: Research (pre-call)
Pull their Google reviews, check the website, find competitor review counts. Build the personalised demo page.
🖥️
Phase 2: The Demo (on the call)
"Before we got on I had a look at your business — I've actually built something." Pull up the URL. Let them explore the AI chat. Ask: how are you currently getting clients? Biggest bottleneck?
🤝
Phase 3: The Ask (close)
Based on what they said, pitch the relevant plan. Send a written proposal within 24 hours. Follow up once. Close or move on.
Questions to ask on the call
"How are you currently getting most of your leads?" "What's your biggest time-waster in the week?" "Do you ever miss calls when you're out on a job?" "When did you last ask a customer for a review?"
