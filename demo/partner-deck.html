The Cold Call

The script.
Keep it short. One goal: the meeting.

1
The hook — 8 seconds to earn 30 more
"Hey, is that [owner name]? I'm [name], I work with Mac AI — we work with Scottish tradespeople to get them more leads without lifting a finger. Quick question — are you the right person to speak to about that?"
2
The gap — make them feel the pain
"Most [roofers / joiners / etc] we speak to are losing 20–30 enquiries a month to missed calls and slow follow-ups. Does that sound familiar?"
3
The pitch — one sentence
"We actually build an AI system that responds to leads instantly, follows up automatically, and builds up your Google reviews — all running in the background while you're out on jobs."
4
The ask — book the call, nothing else
"My colleague Mac has actually put together a quick audit of your business already. Would you be up for a 20-minute call this week to have a look? No pitch, just show you what he found."
5
The close — lock it in
"Brilliant — what works better, [Day] morning or [Day] afternoon?" · Get name, confirm email, send calendar invite. Done.

Mac's Process

The audit call.
Show up prepared. Let the demo do the selling.

Three phases. Total time: 20–30 minutes.

🔍
Phase 1: Research (pre-call)
Pull their Google reviews, check the website, find competitor review counts. Build the personalised demo page.
🖥️
Phase 2: The Demo (on the call)
"Before we got on I had a look at your business — I've actually built something." Pull up the URL. Let them explore the AI chat. Ask: how are you currently getting clients? Biggest bottleneck?
🤝
Phase 3: The Ask (close)
Based on what they said, pitch the relevant plan. Send a written proposal within 24 hours. Follow up once. Close or move on.
Questions to ask on the call
"How are you currently getting most of your leads?" "What's your biggest time-waster in the week?" "Do you ever miss calls when you're out on a job?" "When did you last ask a customer for a review?"
